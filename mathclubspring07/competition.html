The Problem Solving Competition
sponsored by The American Society for Mathematics


Each month the University of Maryland Math Club will post math problems on the bulletin board inside the Math Majors Lounge 0107, and outside the Undergraduate Advising Office 1115. Solutions are to be either emailed to mathclub@math.umd.edu, or turned in to a math club representative. We expect that in August 2006 our best problem solving University of Maryland math major will have the opportunity to compete in the U.S. National Collegiate Mathematics Championship at Mathfest (this year at Tennessee).

We will post the best solutions on the Math Club website. They will be graded not only for their accuracy but also for how well they are expressed.

Please send any questions to the departmental advisor to math club (Sean Lawton) at slawton@math.umd.edu.
