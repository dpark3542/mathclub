If you're looking to join Math Club, there are absolutely no requirements!
You don't even have to be a math major!

Just come to one of our meetings on MONDAYS from 4:00-5:00 PM, in the Undergraduate Math Lounge on the Ground Floor of the Math Building!

In addition, our events are open to all students, whether Math Club members, math majors, or otherwise.
